Wedding Package

Indian weddings are a gigantic affair filled with immense colorful events and a long list of traditions which goes on for days.

Sangeet Nights are full of fun and entertainment for the family and relatives of bride and groom. On this day special weddings songs and dances are performed by them. To make this night more special and memorable ,special singing and dancing performances are performed. If you have a requirement for any dance groups or traditional singing groups, we can arrange that as well. For Sangeet Night we provide all arrangements from DJ Booking to Venue booking, Catering services and decorations.
